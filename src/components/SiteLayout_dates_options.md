# Date Display Location Options

## Option 1: Inside Header (Next to Logo) ✅ RECOMMENDED
**Location:** Inside the header bar, right next to "Masjid Annoor" name
**Pros:** 
- Always visible
- Compact and elegant
- Professional look
- Doesn't take extra vertical space

```
[Logo] Masjid Annoor    [📅 15 Sha'ban | Mar 15]    [Home] [About] [Construction] [Donate]
```

---

## Option 2: Top of Page (Above Header)
**Location:** Very top of website, above the main header
**Pros:**
- Most prominent visibility
- Traditional placement for dates
- Can show more information

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ☪ 15 Sha'ban 1447    |    Sunday, March 15, 2025          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
[Logo] Masjid Annoor Wichita          [Home] [About] [Donate]
```

---

## Option 3: In the Ticker Bar (Combined)
**Location:** Integrated with the moving ticker text
**Pros:**
- Dynamic and eye-catching
- Uses existing space efficiently
- Moving text draws attention

```
[Moving Ticker] ☪ 15 Sha'ban | Mar 15 ✦ A MASJID BEYOND WALLS... ✦ إِنَّ ٱلْجَنَّةَ هِىَ ٱلْمَأْوَى ✦
```

---

## Option 4: Footer (Always Visible)
**Location:** In the footer, always shown at bottom
**Pros:**
- Doesn't clutter header
- Available on all pages
- Can show full detailed dates

```
[Logo] Masjid Annoor          © 2025 Masjid Annoor Wichita
Wichita, Kansas               ☪ 15 Sha'ban 1447 | Sunday, March 15, 2025
```
